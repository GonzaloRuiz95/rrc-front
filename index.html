Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Ruhe Rhino's Club?
R.R.C is a collection of handmade NFTs embedded in the Solana blockchain. Each NFT is a unique piece within the collection and works as a Rude Rhinos Club membership card.
How many Rude Rhinos are in the collection?
The R.R.C. collection consists of 4,000 Rhinos. As the project progresses, the number of the collectibles will decrease since R.R.C. is a deflationary collection. This means your NFT will become more exclusive.
What is the mint price of a Rude Rhino?
0.6 SOL.
Who are the OG members?
They are the first 500 members to join our discord community. We want to reward our OG members, those who put their trust in us from the very beginning, with a series of exclusive advantages.
What are the advantages of OG members?
OG members will be the only ones to know the opening time of the first mint round. They will also be guaranteed to be one of those selected in a future airdrop with limited units.
In what market we will be listed after launch?
We will be listed in MagicEden, AlphaArt, Exchange.art and DigitalEyes.
What will be the percentage of royalties going to the V.I.P Zone/ Community Wallet?

The percentage earmarked for the V.I.P Zone/ Community Wallet will be 50%.

To what actions will the Community Wallet funds be allocated?

- 50% will be used for the deflationary collection

- 30% for DAO

- 20% for the development of airdrops and Metaverse.

You have all the complete information in section 3 of our RoadMap, Tokenomics.
